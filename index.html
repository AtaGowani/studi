What is Studi?

We are your personalized study and workspace.

Studi aims to help users develop healthy work habits. By tracking your emotional health during a Studi Session, we can provide you with feedback on how to optimize the effectiveness of your studying, while also considering your mental well-being. Whether you need help staying on track, reminders to take a break, or even a personalized study plan, we can help you .

Our Process

Facial Recognition

With your permission, we collect data on your mood using Microsoft Azure's Emotion API through your Studi Session. We ensure all information is collected safely - your privacy is our top priority.

Identify Descrepancies

Store data at points indicating a trend in mood. This includes: (1) When mood is detected to be sad, stressed, angry, fearful, or disgusted and (2) When your mood is consistent over a long period of time.

Create User Feedback

Send notifications promoting healthy habits when appropriate (ex. reminder to take a break when prolonged stress is detected). Data collected over sessions will be organized to create a plan optimized for you.
